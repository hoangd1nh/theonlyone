Winter Dressing Inspiration From These Best-Dressed Women

Winter dresses have often been associated with boring styles that are further made to look bulky and uncomfortable because of haphazard layering. There’s no need to wear a cardigan and top it with a trench only to make you look two sizes bigger than you really are. The key is in choosing the right styles with thick fabrics that supplant the need of wearing multiple things.

The fit of the winter dress is also important. We understand nailing cold weather dressing can get particularly tricky, which is why we have rounded up some of the best winter dresses of celebrities who are resident members of our best dressed list. Have a look for inspiration on winter dresses to wear this season!

Swap your run-of-the-mill shirts and silk blouses with soft knit jumpers (also called pullovers) for a look that’s comfortable and warm. They can be dressed for formal purposes and casual outings alike by wearing them with trousers or laidback bottoms like slouchy jeans, joggers, and skirts.

Some jumpers, like Mrunal Thakur’s ochre yellow one here, are dressy and come with knit embossing for some extra texture. Wear them with flared leg denim with frayed hems for a grunge look that’s still soft and winter-appropriate.

Or take inspiration from Olivia Palermo, the fashion influencer, who has paired her oversized turtleneck jumper with a matching pleated midi skirt for a casual day look.

Tip: When buying knit jumpers, it’s best to look for styles that are boxy and oversized instead of body-hugging designs. Wear these styles with your favourite pair of sneakers or boots.
